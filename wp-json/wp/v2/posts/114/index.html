One of the most common fears of anyone who is creating something, whether it\u2019s online content or art or a new business or startup \u2026 is the fear of putting our work out there in the world.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

It\u2019s a fear that has us procrastinating, researching endlessly, checking social media, answering emails, buying books on the topic, listening to podcasts and watching Youtube videos, cleaning our kitchen, deciding the laptop needs a good cleaning \u2026 endlessly!<\/p>\n\n\n\n

None of these things are wrong or bad \u2014 they\u2019re so human! But it\u2019s a fear we have to face at some point if we ever want to make an impact on the world.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

I\u2019d love to share how I work with this fear \u2014 I\u2019ve been doing it for decades now!<\/p>\n\n\n\n

The brief summary:<\/p>\n\n\n\n

  1. I have a litmus test: if I\u2019m not creating or putting the creation out into the world, there\u2019s a good chance I\u2019m procrastinating. (There are exceptions of course.)<\/li>
  2. If I\u2019m avoiding, then I create space to face the fear and create and put it out there.<\/li>
  3. Then I face it. And play with it. And learn to relish it!<\/li><\/ol>\n\n\n\n
